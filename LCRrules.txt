1. Enter the number of players. There must be at least 3 players.
2. Each player starts with 3 chips.
3. The middle has a pot where chips can be placed. These chips are out of the game.
4. In turns, each player will roll a number of die equal to the number of chips they have.
5. If the player rolls a "L", they must pass a chip to the player on their left.
6. If a player rolls a "R", they must pass a chip to the player on their right.
7. If a player rolls a "C", they must place the chip in the middle pot.
8. If a player rolls a "*" die, no action is taken.
9. Take turns rolling dice.
10. The last player with chips remaining wins.
